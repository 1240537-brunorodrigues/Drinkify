How to Use the Digital Hub

  1. Dice Roll: Tap the dice icon at top-left to roll a digital dice (1–6). Use this to advance on your physical board.
  2. Draw a Card: Choose “Draw a Card” to see a randomly chosen card (from the PDF deck). Follow its instructions.
  3. Draw a Question: Choose “Draw a Question” to trigger a multiple-choice question. Answer it; on correct/incorrect, you receive in-game effects (e.g. advance/retreat or “sip” instructions).
  4. User Manual: You’re here! This page explains how to navigate.

General Rules

1. Players take turns rolling the dice (physically on the board) or you can use the digital dice.
2. When landing on a colored space:
  • White = Nothing happens.
  • Blue = “Take a sip” (everyone uses their own drink).
  • Pink = Draw a card (use the digital “Draw a Card” feature).
  • Purple = Draw a question (use the digital “Draw a Question”). If you answer correctly, you gain an advantage; if you’re wrong, you face a penalty.

Please drink responsibly and have fun!
